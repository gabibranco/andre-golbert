About my Studio

I am a professional, classically-trained pianist with a great passion for teaching. My mission as a pedagogue is to instill a love for music and for art in every student. I offer in-home private lessons in the NYC area and online lessons globally.

I am happy to work with students of any level aged four and older. Whether you are a young beginner, an accomplished musician wishing to prepare for conservatoire auditions, an adult beginner, or a lifelong music lover with a knack for piano performance, my studio is the right place for you!

As a Steinway Teacher and Educational Partner, I offer my students a number of educational opportunities, including the chance to perform at Steinway Hall for our studio recitals.

One-on-one lessons

My lessons often go beyond piano technique and musical expression to explore additional topics of musicianship, music theory, history, and aesthetics.

Lessons last typically one hour, except for very young beginners, who can choose between 30 and 45-minute lessons.

Tuition

Tuition will be charged on a monthly basis according to the number of lessons scheduled.

Trial lessons

I offer free online consultations for anyone interested in starting lessons. Please don't hesitate to reach out and sign up here!

Cancellation Policy

I kindly ask that you send cancellation requests with a minimum of 24 hours in advance. If a cancellation is made less than 24 hours before the scheduled time, the lesson will be charged. Exceptions apply for serious and unforeseen circumstances.

Make-up lessons on alternative dates and times can be arranged as needed!
